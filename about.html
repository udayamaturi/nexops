Our Story

Who We Are

Founded on the principle that technology should drive business forward, NexOps IT Solutions has spent 15 years delivering strategic efficiency and digital strength to organizations worldwide.

Mission

Empowering Business Through Technology

Our mission is to empower organizations with transformative BPO and IT solutions that reduce complexity, optimize costs, and accelerate growth. We serve as an extension of your team — deeply invested in your success.

Every solution we deliver is built around measurable outcomes, transparent processes, and long-term partnership — because your success is our reputation.

Vision

The Future of Enterprise Operations

We envision a world where businesses of all sizes have access to enterprise-grade IT capabilities — where geography is no barrier to operational excellence, and where innovation is within every company's reach.

By 2030, we aim to be the leading global partner for digital-first business operations across 60+ countries.

Our Journey

15 Years of Growing Stronger

From a small IT consultancy to a global BPO and technology powerhouse — our story is one of relentless growth and unwavering commitment to excellence.

2010

Founded in Chicago

NexOps was established as a boutique IT consulting firm with a team of 12 engineers and a vision for smarter enterprise technology.

2013

BPO Division Launched

Expanded into Business Process Outsourcing, offering customer support and data processing services to mid-market clients.

2016

Global Expansion

Opened offices in London, Singapore, and Hyderabad. Crossed 100 enterprise clients across 25 countries.

2020

Digital Transformation Practice

Launched dedicated cloud migration and digital transformation practice, helping 80+ clients navigate remote-first operations.

2025

500+ Clients, 40+ Countries

Today, NexOps serves over 500 clients globally with 1,200+ team members delivering excellence every day.
