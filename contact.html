FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you onboard a new BPO team? +

Typically, we can have a small support team (5–20 agents) fully onboarded and operational within 2–4 weeks. Larger engagements may take 6–8 weeks to ensure proper training and quality standards.

Do you offer flexible engagement models? +

Yes. We offer project-based, retainer, and dedicated team models across all our services — both BPO and IT consulting. We'll work with you to find the structure that fits your budget and objectives.

How do you ensure data security? +

We are ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2 Type II compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We sign comprehensive NDAs and DPAs with every client and conduct regular third-party security audits.

What industries do you specialize in? +

We serve clients across Finance & Banking, Healthcare, Retail & E-Commerce, Manufacturing, Telecom, and Travel & Logistics. Our industry-specific expertise allows us to deliver solutions that meet sector-specific compliance and operational requirements.
